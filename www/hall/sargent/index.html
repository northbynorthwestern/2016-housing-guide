Sargent Hall

2245 Sheridan Road

Perhaps best known for its dining hall, Sargent Hall on North Campus houses mostly freshman and sophomores. Sargent-living makes it easy to roll right out of bed and head to Tech for a 9 a.m or make it to the fraternity quad in under a minute. The dining hall serves as the main one on North campus and is also conveniently located a minute-walk away from Henry Crown Sports Pavilion fitness center. Although it is the best go-to for food on North Campus, there is still ongoing debate between whether Sargent or Allison has better dining.

What residents say

“I’m an engineer and we’re really close to Tech, so I can just roll out of bed five minutes before class and make it there through the little basement tunnel. We only have a lounge and a kitchen on one floor, and I know one flight of stairs is not a lot but it’s just irritating.”

Millie Rosen, McCormick freshman

“I love the people, and I say I don’t like living on north campus because it’s far from south campus, but it’s literally four blocks. I love living in Sargent and I love that it’s close to SPAC.”

Sam van Loon, SoC freshman

“I love the overhead storage. That is one hunnit my favorite.”

Danielle Hojnicki, Weinberg freshman

What non-residents say

“ My dad did his undergrad here at Northwestern and he lived in Sargent. He said it hasn’t changed since then.”

Griff Bryant, Weinberg freshman

“ My impressions of it before were like, no matter what, don’t go to Sarge, it’s the worst place to live. But my friend’s room in Sarge is better than my room in Bobb. Maybe it depends on the room. I definitely don’t think it’s as bad as they made it out to be.”

Nathan Reiff, Weinberg freshman
